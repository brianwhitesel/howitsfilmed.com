# Email Sequence — Free Signup to HIF Pro Upgrade

**Date drafted:** 2026-05-18
**Audience:** Anyone who claims a free HIF account at howitsfilmed.com/app (the highest-intent inbound segment)
**Sequence length:** 3 emails over 4 days
**Funnel goal:** free tier → HIF Pro on Whop. Free signup is the hottest possible lead — they have already evaluated the brand and crossed the "give my email" threshold. The next 96 hours is the cleanest window to convert them before the dopamine of "I just got a useful free tool" fades.
**Why this angle:** Yesterday's run hit top-of-funnel (IG carousel for the 175K audience). The middle of the funnel — free signup to paid — has no automated sequence yet. Free-to-paid is the fastest revenue lever the loop can pull toward the May 31 $5K target because the audience is already self-qualified.

---

## Sending notes (read before deploying)

- **Where to send from:** Whop's built-in email if it supports automations on signup, otherwise ConvertKit / Klaviyo / whatever is wired up. Trigger on free-tier signup event.
- **Suppression:** exclude any address that already has a Starter / Creator / Pro tag. No one wants three upgrade emails after they already paid.
- **From name:** Brian at How It's Filmed
- **Reply-to:** brianwhitesel@gmail.com so replies actually reach a human
- **Voice check:** first-name greeting, no em-dashes, no emojis, plain URLs. If any phrase below feels generic when read on a phone, cut it.
- **Send times (ET):** Email 1 immediate, Email 2 at 9am the next morning, Email 3 at 9am two days after that. This spaces the cadence so it does not feel like a barrage.

## What success looks like

- Email 1 open rate above 60 percent. It is a transactional welcome, so the floor is high.
- Email 3 click-through to /app above 8 percent. That's the email that does the actual conversion work.
- Free-to-paid conversion above 4 percent over a 14-day window from signup. Below 2 percent means the sequence is not earning the upgrade and Email 2 needs a stronger story or Email 3 needs a sharper offer.
