The Story

Why we exist.

How It's Filmed started as a simple idea: the systems that professional productions run on shouldn't be locked behind a $50,000 film school education or years of industry access. A creator with a camera and the right tools can produce work that looks like it cost ten times what it did.

We built over 100 production tools — shot lists, call sheets, budget trackers, color notes, client proposals, gear checklists — and put them on the internet for free. Then we kept building.

The toolkit is live at howitsfilmed.com/app. It grows every week. Everything is built by working filmmakers who use these tools on real productions.

"Film like a pro. Post like one too. You don't have to choose."
— How It's Filmed
