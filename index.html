What is How It's Filmed

Film school skips the part you actually need.

How It's Filmed reverse-engineers the shots you stop scrolling for — the lighting, the movement, the edit — and turns them into things you can use on your next shoot. No theory dumps. No gear worship. Just how it's actually filmed.

  • ✓Breakdowns, not lectures. See exactly how a shot was lit, rigged and moved — then steal it.
  • ✓Tools that ship with you. Templates, references and workflows built for working shooters.
  • ✓A real crew. 175K+ filmmakers comparing notes, not competing for clout.
