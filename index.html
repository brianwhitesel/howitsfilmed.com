The HIF Method

FIVE STEPS. ONE SYSTEM.

How It's Filmed is built around a sequence, not a list of tips. The FRAME method is the framework behind everything we teach and build.

F
Find Your Story
Every shot needs a reason to exist. Start with the story, not the camera.
R
Right Gear Mindset
The camera doesn't make the filmmaker. Use what you already own.
A
Audio First
Bad audio kills good video. Capture clean sound every time.
M
Movement with Purpose
Intentional motion communicates. Random movement distracts.
E
Edit with Intention
The edit is where the film is made. Cut with confidence and finish.
